ABOUT ME

I'm Melanie Ruddock. I graduated from Montclair State University in January 2021 with a major in Television Production and a minor in Business. Growing up, my dad would turn on the camera to take photos or record every little event to add to our collection of home videos. Eventually, I decided that I wanted to be behind the camera for a change and once I picked up the camera I never put it back down. The more I learned about camera work and other aspects of television production, the more I knew this was something I needed to be a part of. The ability to be creative is one of the most alluring aspects of TV, and I use that ability at every opportunity available to me. Not only that, but I love to work with others. Television production takes a lot of talented people behind the scenes and being able to learn from, as well as help crew members makes the job that much more rewarding. Besides my life in the world of media, I enjoy photography, sketching, playing the piano and spending time with my friends and family. No matter what I am doing, I will always give it my all.
